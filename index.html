3. How We Process Your Data

3.1 Email Scanning Process

When you use the Inbox Scanner feature:

  • Your device connects directly to your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, or iCloud) via IMAP using the credentials you provide.
  • Email content is sent to our processing server solely for the purpose of extracting order information (product names, prices, quantities, and order numbers).
  • Our server processes the email content in real-time and returns the extracted data to your device.
  • No email content, credentials, or extracted data is stored, logged, or retained on our server after processing is complete.
  • The connection between your device and our server is encrypted.

3.2 PSA Card Lookups

When you use the PSA slab features, the app may connect to the PSA API to retrieve grading information for your cards. Only the card's certification number is sent; no personal data is transmitted.

3.3 Background Email Scanning

If you enable the auto-scan feature for an inbox, the app may periodically check for new order emails in the background using iOS Background App Refresh. This uses the same secure process described above. You can enable or disable this per inbox at any time.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All inventory, sales, and personal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.
  • Email passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain, which uses hardware-level encryption.
  • Non-sensitive settings (inbox configuration, scan preferences) are stored in UserDefaults on your device.
  • We do not operate user accounts or cloud storage. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

5. Data Sharing

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Deletion: You can delete any individual item, inbox, or record within the app. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.
  • Email scanning: The Inbox Scanner is entirely optional. You can add or remove inboxes at any time, and toggle background scanning on or off per inbox.
  • Credentials: You can remove saved email credentials at any time by deleting the inbox from the app.
  • Scan history: You can clear the "already saved" history at any time to re-scan previously processed emails.

7. Children's Privacy

SalEZ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. We do not retain any data on our servers. When email content is processed for order extraction, it is handled in memory and discarded immediately after the response is sent to your device.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
